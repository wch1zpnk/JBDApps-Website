Support

Help for JBDApps software.

For the fastest help, include the app name, app version, macOS or iOS version, and a clear description of what happened.

Getting Help

Email support@JBDApps.com with the details below. Support is handled directly by the developer.

What to Include

  • App name and version number
  • Device model and macOS or iOS version
  • Steps that led to the issue
  • Screenshots or exact error messages, if useful

Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I find app privacy information?

Use the Privacy Policy page. Each app's policy should be reviewed before App Store submission and updated when data practices change.

Where is the Mac App Store link?

The Everything Clipboard Mac App Store link is marked as coming soon until publication is complete and the public listing can be verified.

Does Everything Clipboard require Accessibility permission?

Some direct paste workflows may ask for Accessibility permission. Basic clipboard history and copy workflows should remain separate from that optional permission.
