JBDApps Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 16, 2026

This policy describes privacy practices for JBDApps software and this website. It will be updated when app behavior, support practices, or platform requirements change.

Data Collection

JBDApps apps are intended to be privacy-conscious utilities. Actual data collection depends on the specific app and enabled features, and each public app should be reviewed against its real behavior.

Data Usage

When an app stores information locally, that information is used to provide the app's features on the user's device. JBDApps does not use this website to collect analytics, advertising identifiers, or tracking cookies.

Third-Party Services

Some app features may contact Apple services, such as App Store pages or update lookups. App Store downloads, purchases, and review flows are handled by Apple under Apple's own terms and privacy practices.

Analytics

This website does not include analytics scripts by default. If analytics are added later, this policy should be updated before those tools are enabled.

Crash Logs

Apps distributed through Apple platforms may make crash information available through Apple developer tools when users have enabled sharing with developers. Support requests may also include screenshots, logs, or descriptions that users choose to send.

Everything Clipboard

Everything Clipboard is designed around local clipboard history workflows. Clipboard history and app settings should remain on the user's Mac unless the user chooses to export, share, or send information for support. Optional direct paste workflows may require Accessibility permission. Update checks may contact Apple's App Store service and should not send clipboard history or saved app data to JBDApps.

Voice Command Atlas

The Mac App Store version of Voice Command Atlas reads only the .voicecontrolcommands export the user explicitly chooses in a standard macOS Open dialog. It may securely remember that selection with an app-scoped security bookmark. Edits affect only the selected export until the user re-imports it through Voice Control's Options menu. Command contents, backups, snapshots, and test results stay on the Mac and are not uploaded to JBDApps. The app does not read another app's preferences or restart Voice Control or other system processes.

Children's Privacy

JBDApps apps are not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through a support request, contact JBDApps so it can be handled appropriately.

Contact Information

For privacy questions, email support@JBDApps.com.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated as apps, support practices, or App Store requirements change. The effective date will be updated when material changes are made.
